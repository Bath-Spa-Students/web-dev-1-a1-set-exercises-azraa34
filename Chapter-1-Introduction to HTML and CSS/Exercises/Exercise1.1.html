Chocolate Brownies

This is a recipe for rich and fudgy chocolate brownies that includes a secret ingredient for extra moistness and decadence. Each bite offers a delightful blend of dense, chocolatey goodness and a satisfyingly gooey texture. Whether you're craving a comforting snack, preparing for a special gathering, or simply treating yourself, these brownies are a true indulgence. With their deep cocoa flavor and melt-in-your-mouth consistency, they are bound to become your go-to dessert favorite. They're perfect for any occasion, whether you want a sweet snack or a dessert that impresses.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (240g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup (200g) unsalted butter
  • 8 ounces (225g) semisweet chocolate, chopped
  • 1 1/2 cups (300g) granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup (120g) chopped walnuts or pecans (optional)

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x13-inch baking pan.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.
  3. In a large microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter and chocolate together in the microwave, stirring every 30 seconds until smooth.
  4. Whisk in the sugar until combined.
  5. Whisk in the eggs, one at a time, until well combined.
  6. Whisk in the vanilla extract.
  7. Fold in the dry ingredients until just combined. Do not overmix.
  8. If using, fold in the chopped nuts.
  9. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and spread evenly.
  10. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.
  11. Remove from the oven and allow to cool completely in the pan on a wire rack.
  12. Cut into squares and serve.
