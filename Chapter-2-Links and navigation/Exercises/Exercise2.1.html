Dead Plate

Dead Plate is a 2D indie horror game developed by RachelDrawsThis and Ekrix, featuring composition and sound design by BKalengar. The game was created using RPG Maker and combines classic restaurant tycoon mechanics with visual novel elements and point-and-click exploration.

Plot

Set in 1960s France, Dead Plate follows the story of Rody, a young man hired to work at a fancy bistro by the stoic but charismatic chef named Vince. Despite Vince's reservations about Rody's job retention, Rody is determined to make as much money as he can in a week. However, as the days go on, Rody starts having violent dreams and becomes suspicious of his boss and the food served at the bistro.

Characters

Rody Lamoree

Rody Lamoree is the protagonist of Dead Plate. He is determined to earn as much money as possible working at Vince's bistro, despite having a questionable job history.

Vincent "Vince" Charbonneau

Vincent Charbonneau is the stoic and charismatic chef who owns the fancy bistro where Rody works. He becomes a source of suspicion for Rody as strange events unfold.

Marieanne "Manon" Vacher

Marieanne "Manon" Vacher is Rody's ex-girlfriend and adds complexity to Rody's character and storyline.

Endings

Spoiler: Endings

Dead Plate has 4 different endings. To unlock Endings 2-4, players must trigger the Phone Call event that occurs on the night of Day 6.

To achieve certain endings, specific actions need to be taken during gameplay:

Requirements:

  • Take out trash on Day 3 (Cutscene)
  • Walk in the kitchen past a minute mark on Day 4 (Cutscene)
  • Talk with Vince on Day 4 ("Can you give me a raise" and "Do you like your job" options)
  • Talk with Vince on Day 5 ("What's your favorite food")
  • Go to Vince's office during Day 5 Night Restaurant segment and interact with the trash can
  • Get the phone call on the night of Day 6 and answer yes

Ending 1: Table for One

Complete shifts entirely from Day 1 to Day 7.

Ending 2: Best-Served Cold

Start Day 7 shift without equipping Matches. When the shift starts, enter the freezer.

Ending 3: Best-Served Hot

Start Day 7 shift with Matches equipped. When the shift starts, enter the freezer.

Ending 4: Abattoir

Fail any of the Quick Time Events (QTE) during the final chase.
