Plot

Set in 1960s France, Dead Plate follows the story of Rody, a young man hired to work at a fancy bistro by the stoic but charismatic chef named Vince. Despite Vince's reservations about Rody's job retention, Rody is determined to make as much money as he can in a week. However, as the days go on, Rody starts having violent dreams and becomes suspicious of his boss and the food served at the bistro.
