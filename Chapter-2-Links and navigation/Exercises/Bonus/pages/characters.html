Characters

Rody Lamoree

Rody Lamoree is the protagonist of Dead Plate. He is determined to earn as much money as possible working at Vince's bistro, despite having a questionable job history.

Vincent "Vince" Charbonneau

Vincent Charbonneau is the stoic and charismatic chef who owns the fancy bistro where Rody works. He becomes a source of suspicion for Rody as strange events unfold.

Marieanne "Manon" Vacher

Marieanne "Manon" Vacher is Rody's ex-girlfriend and adds complexity to Rody's character and storyline.
